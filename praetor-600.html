Overview

Disruptive Performance

The Embraer Praetor 600 shatters the boundaries of the super mid-size category. With a range of 4,018 nautical miles, it connects Montreal to London or Lisbon non-stop—capabilities previously reserved for much larger aircraft.

Featuring Embraer's exclusive turbulence reduction technology and the quietest cabin in its class, the Praetor 600 delivers an unmatched passenger experience. The stand-up cabin with six-foot headroom accommodates up to 12 passengers in true comfort.
