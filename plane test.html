Overview

A Proven Performer

The Bombardier Challenger 604 has earned its reputation as the workhorse of business aviation. With over 1,000 aircraft delivered, it remains the benchmark for reliability and performance in the super mid-size category.

Featuring a flat-floor cabin standing 6.1 feet tall, the 604 offers true comfort for up to 10 passengers on flights up to 3,850 nautical miles. The spacious interior includes a full galley, private lavatory, and ample baggage compartment accessible in-flight.
